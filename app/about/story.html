The Story of Oxygen

Cycles have always played a crucial means of transport in our vast country and will continue to be one in future also. Although the purpose of cycling is taking a dynamic shift, from being an important mode of transport to being part of a healthy lifestyle, the bicycle can never be excluded from our lifecycle. In this widening of the purposes of a cycle there are many manufacturers who are focused only on appearance of a cycle, whereas the quality and affordability to meet the Indian needs where being unmet.

We as a manufacturer took this as our mission to produce cycles which are modern as well as durable and the one can be choice of cycle for every Indian. We named Our brand as Oxygen to give the portray the importance of cycles in our lives, we need oxygen to breathe and live our life and cycle is also something which has same kind of significance also due to the ever growing pollution the oxygen we breathe is becoming unsafe and cycling is something which can help us clean our environment and therefore get a better oxygen to breathe.
With these brand purpose and a vision in my mind we launched our brand J.J. Cycles in the year 2009 and from there on we have improvised and constantly worked on various versions of cycles ranging from Kids cycles to adults.
Our Kids models like Stoppy, Skoda, sun spider have all the elements which children love and our brands like Brocode and Ultimate are made for the young teenagers.
